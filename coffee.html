Description:

Every month at Everproof, two team members are assigned a coffee catchup. Born out of our first hackathon, the tech team aimed to build a plugin for Twist (a messaging app) which could fully automate these catchups. My task was to build an algorithm which best determined matchups, and integrate this with Twist's API.

Technologies used:

  • Python
  • Twist (messaging app)
  • Postman (API Development tool)

Algorithm design:

I designed the algorithm in Python. The algorithm would determine the best matchups by scoring all potential matchups, (based on a variety of factors), and selecting the most favourable coffee catchups.

These factors included:

  • The time since their last catchup together
  • If they were from the same department (eg. Sales, Support, Engineering)
  • The times they had caught up with other members

I took great pride in clear and consise code, so that my team members could understand the algorithm in a quick time period.

Intergration with TwistAPI:

As I finished my algorithm earlier than expected, I began intergrating with TwistAPI. I used Postman to test posting to the API.

I linked the notifications to the algorithm through a post_notifications() function - every month when the algorithm ran, the notifications were instantaneously posted to Twist.
